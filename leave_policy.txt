COMPREHENSIVE HR LEAVE POLICY DOCUMENT

Page 1: Introduction, Purpose & Applicability
1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this Leave Policy is to establish a structured and transparent framework for managing employee leave across the organization. This policy aims to:
Promote employee well-being and work-life balance
Ensure operational continuity
Maintain fairness and consistency in leave administration
Provide clarity on entitlements, procedures, and compliance requirements
1.2 Applicability
This policy applies to:
All full-time employees
Part-time employees (on a pro-rata basis)
The policy does not automatically apply to:
Contractual staff
Interns or consultants
Unless explicitly stated in their respective agreements.
1.3 Definitions
Leave Year: January 1st to December 31st
Working Days: Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays
Immediate Family: Parents, spouse, children, and siblings
1.4 General Guidelines
All leave requests must be submitted via the official HRMS platform
Leave is subject to managerial approval based on business needs
Employees must ensure proper handover before proceeding on leave
Unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action
Page 2: Standard Leave Entitlements
2.1 Casual Leave (CL)
Casual Leave is granted for short-term personal needs.
Entitlement:
12 days per calendar year
Rules:
Cannot be carried forward to the next year
Maximum 3 consecutive days allowed
Requires prior approval except in emergencies
2.2 Sick Leave (SL)
Sick Leave is granted in case of illness or medical conditions.
Entitlement:
10 days per calendar year
Rules:
Medical certificate required for leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
Cannot be encashed or carried forward
May be combined with Earned Leave if extended illness occurs
2.3 Earned Leave (EL) / Privilege Leave
Earned Leave is accrued based on days worked.
Entitlement:
18 days per calendar year
Rules:
Can be carried forward up to a maximum of 45 days
Can be availed for planned long-duration leave
Eligible for encashment during separation or as per company policy
Page 3: Special Leave Categories
3.1 Maternity Leave
Eligible female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid leave
Applicable after confirmation of employment
Can be taken before and after childbirth as per legal provisions
3.2 Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to 5 working days
Must be availed within 3 months of childbirth
3.3 Bereavement Leave
3 days of paid leave in case of death of an immediate family member
Additional leave may be granted at management discretion
3.4 Marriage Leave
5 working days of leave for an employee’s own marriage
Can be combined with Earned Leave
3.5 Leave Without Pay (LWP)
Applicable when all leave balances are exhausted
Requires approval from both manager and HR
Salary deductions will apply accordingly
Page 4: Leave Application & Approval Process
4.1 Application Process
Planned leave must be applied at least 3 working days in advance
For extended leave (>5 days), 7 days prior notice is recommended
4.2 Emergency Leave
Must be communicated via email, call, or HRMS notification
Formal application must be submitted post-facto
4.3 Approval Workflow
Employee submits request
Reporting Manager reviews request
Approval/Rejection with comments
HRMS updates leave balance automatically
4.4 Leave Cancellation & Modification
Leave can be canceled or modified prior to commencement
Requires manager approval
4.5 Leave During Notice Period
Strongly discouraged
Approval subject to business exigencies
Unused Earned Leave may be adjusted against notice period where applicable
Page 5: Festival Holidays & Company-Declared Holidays
5.1 Public & Festival Holidays
The organization observes a set of public and festival holidays each calendar year in accordance with regional and national guidelines.
Key Points:
A holiday calendar will be published at the beginning of each year
Holidays may vary based on location and operational requirements
Employees may be allowed to choose from a list of optional/restricted holidays
5.2 Optional (Restricted) Holidays
Employees may select up to 2 optional holidays from a predefined list of cultural or religious festivals
Requests must be submitted in advance and are subject to approval
5.3 Working on a Holiday
In cases where employees are required to work on a declared holiday:
Compensatory Off (Comp-Off) will be provided
Comp-Off must be availed within 30 days of accrual
Manager approval is mandatory
5.4 Holiday During Leave
If a declared holiday falls within an approved leave period, it will not be counted as a leave day
Page 6: Compliance, Encashment & Policy Governance
5.1 Leave Encashment
Only Earned Leave is eligible
Processed during Full & Final settlement
Encashment value based on last drawn salary
5.2 Public Holidays During Leave
Public holidays falling within leave period are not counted as leave days
5.3 Misuse of Leave Policy
Providing false information or misuse may result in disciplinary action
Repeated violations may lead to escalation
5.4 Exceptions & Discretion
Exceptions may be granted in special circumstances
Requires approval from HR and senior management
5.5 Policy Review & Updates
This policy is subject to periodic review
Changes will be communicated through official channels
